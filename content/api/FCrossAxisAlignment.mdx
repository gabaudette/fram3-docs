# FCrossAxisAlignment

Controls how children are aligned along the cross axis of a FColumn (horizontal) or FRow (vertical). Maps to UIToolkit Align values.

## Fields

### Center

Center children along the cross axis.

### End

Align children toward the end of the cross axis.

### Start

Align children toward the start of the cross axis.

### Stretch

Stretch children to fill the cross axis.
