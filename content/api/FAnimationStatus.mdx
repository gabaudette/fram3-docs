# FAnimationStatus

Describes the current playback state of an FAnimationController.

## Fields

### Completed

The animation has finished and progress is at its final value.

### Forward

The animation is actively advancing forward toward 1.

### Idle

The animation is not running and the progress is at the beginning.

### Reverse

The animation is actively advancing in reverse toward 0.
